Projects & Walkthroughs

Welcome to the Projects and Walkthroughs section of my cybersecurity portfolio. This space is dedicated to showcasing practical applications of my skills, methodologies, and analytical approaches within the realm of Security Operations Center (SOC) activities. From log analysis and alert triage to threat detection, incident response, and threat intelligence correlation, each project reflects my technical proficiency, attention to detail, and commitment to proactive defense and continuous improvement in operational security.

In this section, you'll find a curated collection of:

Hands-On Projects

Realistic scenarios and simulations that demonstrate the application of cybersecurity tools, techniques, and frameworks. These include virtual lab environments, custom scripts, security configurations, and vulnerability assessments.

CTF Write-Ups

Detailed walkthroughs of Capture The Flag (CTF) challenges from platforms such as CyberDefenders, TryHackMe, and other cybersecurity labs. Each write-up includes reconnaissance steps, exploitation paths, privilege escalation methods, and post-exploitation insights.

Incident Response & Threat Hunting Scenarios

Simulated cases of security breaches and threat detections that highlight investigation techniques, log analysis, and containment strategies.

Security Tool Development

Custom scripts and tools developed for automating tasks, performing security testing, or enhancing monitoring capabilities.

Defensive Strategies

Projects focusing on network defense, firewall configurations, SIEM implementations, and system hardening following best practices and compliance standards.

Each walkthrough is structured to emphasize clarity, methodology, and the rationale behind every action taken. Whether you're a fellow cybersecurity enthusiast, a recruiter, or someone simply interested in the field, this section is designed to provide a transparent and informative view into how I approach cybersecurity challenges.

Feel free to explore the projects, dive into the walkthroughs, and reach out with any questions or feedback.
